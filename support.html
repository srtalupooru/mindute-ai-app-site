❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes! Mindute AI runs 100% on your device. No messages, files, or personal data are ever sent to any server. Everything stays on your phone.

Do I need internet to use the app?

No. Once you download a model, everything works completely offline. You only need internet for the initial model download.

How do I download a model?

Go to the Models screen, browse available models, and tap "Download" on any model you want. Downloads typically take 2-10 minutes depending on model size and your connection.

Why is the app using so much storage?

AI models are large files (0.7GB - 2.5GB each). You can manage your models in the Models screen and delete any you're not using to free up space.

Can I use my own models?

Currently, Mindute AI supports curated GGUF models from our registry. We're working on adding custom model support in a future update.

How do I backup my conversations?

Go to Settings → DATA → "Backup all conversations". You can share your backup via email, AirDrop, or save it to your Files app.
